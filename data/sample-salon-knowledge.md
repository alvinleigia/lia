## Parking

Free customer parking is available behind the salon building. Guests can enter the parking area from Palm Grove Avenue.

Street parking is also available nearby, but it may be limited during evenings and weekends. Guests are advised to arrive 10 minutes early if they need parking.

Accessible parking spaces are available near the rear entrance.

## Public Transport

The salon is a 5-minute walk from Downtown Central Station. Bus routes 12, 18, and 44 stop within two blocks of the salon.

## Arrival Guidance

Guests should arrive 10 minutes before their appointment. This allows time for check-in, consultation, and parking.

If a guest is running late, they should call the salon as soon as possible. Arrivals more than 15 minutes late may require the appointment to be shortened or rescheduled.

## Services

The salon offers services across these categories:

- Hair
- Nails
- Makeup

Common services include women's haircut, hair color, blow dry, manicure, pedicure, event makeup, and bridal makeup.

Some color and bridal services may require a consultation before booking.

## Consultations

Consultations are recommended for:

- Hair color changes
- Bridal makeup
- Corrective color
- First-time guests requesting major style changes

Consultations help the stylist understand the guest's hair condition, desired outcome, timing, and pricing expectations.

## Booking Policy

Appointments can be requested online, by phone, or through the chat assistant. A booking request is not final until the salon confirms availability.

Guests should provide:

- Desired service
- Preferred date
- Preferred time
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number

The salon team will contact the guest if the requested time is unavailable.

## Cancellation Policy

Guests should cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before the appointment.

Late cancellations or no-shows may affect future booking priority. For long services such as hair color or bridal makeup, a deposit may be required.

## Payment

The salon accepts:

- Credit cards
- Debit cards
- Contactless payments
- Cash

Tips are appreciated but optional.

## Accessibility

The salon has step-free access through the rear entrance. Accessible parking is available behind the building.

Guests with accessibility requirements are encouraged to mention them when booking so the salon can prepare accordingly.

## Children And Guests

Guests may bring one companion if needed, but seating is limited. Children should be supervised at all times.

For safety reasons, children are not allowed near chemical service areas unless they are receiving a service.

## Pets

Only service animals are allowed inside the salon.

## Wi-Fi

Complimentary guest Wi-Fi is available. Guests can ask the front desk for the current Wi-Fi password.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended. Walk-ins may be accepted depending on staff availability, but they are not guaranteed.

### Is parking available?

Yes. Free customer parking is available behind the salon building.

### Can I book hair color without a consultation?

Simple color services may be booked directly. Major color changes, corrective color, and first-time color guests should book a consultation first.

### How early should I arrive?

Guests should arrive 10 minutes before their appointment.

### What happens if I am late?

If a guest is more than 15 minutes late, the appointment may need to be shortened or rescheduled.

### Are bridal services available?

Yes. Bridal makeup is available, and a trial appointment is recommended before the wedding day.

### Can I request a specific stylist?

Yes. Guests may request a specific stylist, but availability depends on the stylist's schedule.

### Are deposits required?

Deposits may be required for long appointments, bridal services, or high-demand dates.
